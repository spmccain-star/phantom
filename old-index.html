Option 2
The Lots
We'll be here

Starting around 6 PM, corps warm up in the parking lots around the Alamodome in partial uniform. You'll see the horn lines and snare lines running drills separately — not the full show, but a cool behind-the-scenes look at how it all comes together.

You're free to roam and listen to different sections up close. Multiple corps will be doing the same thing. Phantom wears bright red — hard to miss. Once we find them, we'll text everyone a pin so you can find us!

If you're joining us, let us know — once we get a head count we can figure out if dinner together works out after!

Bring a chair if you want to sit
